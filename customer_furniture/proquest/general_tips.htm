General Tips
 
  • Navigation
    Do not use the back button on the browser, users must use the Navigation buttons within the site, otherwise the page will expire, and your place in the system will be lost.
  •  Security
    You will automatically be logged out of the system if your order is left open on your computer for more than 20 minutes.
  • Confirmation
    Every user will receive an order confirmation e-mail when the order is received by Sprint International.
  •  Delivery
    If you expect your delivery to have been delivered and the track and trace shows that it has not been please contact Joanne Uttley.  Please allow up to 5 days for delivery before contacting Marketing.
  • Fees
    We pay a processing fee for every order – please try to think ahead and consolidate orders as much as possible.
