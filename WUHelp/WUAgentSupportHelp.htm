HOW TO USE THE AGENT SUPPORT FACILITY

The Agent Support tab lets you request assistance from Western Union and view replies.

Each request is referred to as a Conversation and can include several messages between you and Western Union.

ABOUT CONVERSATIONS

A Conversation is similar to an email exchange between you and Western Union. Previous and current Conversations are listed on the Agent Support tab. The most recent conversation is shown at the top.

A conversation has
  • a Title (or Topic),
  • a date, the date on which the Conversation was started,
  • optionally a Consignment number if the Conversation refers to a specific consignment,
  • a unique Conversation Reference code and
  • a Status - OPEN or CLOSED.
TO START A NEW CONVERSATION

First ensure your enquiry is not answered in the list of Frequently Asked Questions. If it isn't, start a new conversation.

Click the new conversation button.

Select a title for your conversation from the dropdown box. If your enquiry is not well described by any of the available titles, select Other, then enter your own title in the Other title free text box that appears.

If your enquiry is about an existing consignment, click add consignment reference.  Your last 10 consignments are displayed.  Select the relevant consignment.

In the Raised by text box enter the name of the person who we should contact regarding this enquiry.

In the Message text box, enter your enquiry.

Click the send button. Your message is sent  to Western Union, and a message is returned to indicate that your message has been received successfully. At this point you can add a further message or return to the list of Conversations.

TO VIEW THE MESSAGES IN A CONVERSATION
You will see a red alert at the top of your screen if you have an incoming message waiting, In the list of Conversations, any Conversation with an unread message will also be highlighted.

Click on the blue 'page' icon to view the messages in a conversation.

TO REPLY TO A MESSAGE

Click the green 'plus' icon next to the conversation. Enter your message. Click the send button.

Note that you can add or change the consignment reference at any time.

TO CLOSE A CONVERSATION

If the conversation is finished, mark it CLOSED by clicking the orange 'handshake' icon.

You will be asked for feedback on how we dealt with your enquiry. Select OK or Unsatisfactory in the Rating column.

Either you or the Western Union Controller can decide to close a conversation. Depending on which party closes the message, and whether your feedback is required, you will see one of the following messages:

  AGENT REQUESTED CLOSE
  AGENT REQUESTED CLOSE, AWAITING FEEDBACK
  CLOSED BY AGENT
  CONTROLLER IS CLOSING, AWAITING AGENT FEEDBACK
  CLOSED BY CONTROLLER

When you click the OK or Unsatisfactory button, a screen appears on which you can provide feedback.

In the Closed by text box enter your name.

In the Comments text box enter any additional text you would like Western Union to see.  The Western Union controller will review your comments, and they may be used for training and quality monitoring purposes.

Finally, click the finish button.
