HOW TO USE THE QUICK ORDER FACILITY

The Quick Order  tab lets you place orders using a streamlined interface designed for ease of use.

1.  . View a list of the products you can order by clicking in the search bar. You can list of All available products, or the Most Popular products - use the option buttons above the search bar to select the list you require.

You can narrow down the list by typing into the search bar the name of a  product, or its product code, if you know the code, 

Click on the product you want to add to your basket.

To clear your current search, click the clear search link.

2.  Once you have clicked on a product in the search bar, you can select the quantity you require. The maximum quantity available to you is shown next to the product.

Select the quantity you require and click the Add to Order button.

3.  Repeat steps 1 & 2 until your basket contains all the items you require. Click the X next to a product to remove it from your basket.

4.  Ensure the delivery address shown on the right hand side is correct (if we don't yet have your address on our system no address is shown, and you will be prompted to enter it).

Add your contact name and telephone number, and any Special Instructions.

5.  Click the Place Order button.  The system will ask you to confirm that you want to place this order. If you do, click the OK button

6.  Your order is placed. Make a note of the consignment number that is displayed.  You can check the status of your order by entering the consignment number into the Track and Trace tab.
